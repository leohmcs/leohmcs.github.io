Hello! I am Leo, an Electrical Engineering student at [UFMG](https://ufmg.br/), in Brazil :brazil:. During my Bachelor's, I also spent a wonderful semester abroad at [KTH Royal Institute of Technology](https://www.kth.se/en) in Stockholm, Sweden :sweden:.

I am currently a [Robotics Institute Summer Scholars](https://riss.ri.cmu.edu/) (RISS) intern at Carnegie Mellon University working on safe robot navigation with human-feedback at the [Intent Lab](https://cmu-intentlab.github.io/) with Prof. [Andrea Bajcsy](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~abajcsy/). At UFMG, I was part of the Computer Vision and Robotics Lab ([VeRLab](https://www.verlab.dcc.ufmg.br/)), where I worked on planning for multi-robot systems advised by Prof. [Douglas Macharet](https://homepages.dcc.ufmg.br/~doug/site/en/). In the Summer 2023, I worked as a Research Intern in the [AirLab](https://theairlab.org/) at Carnegie Mellon University also as part of the RISS program advised by [Brady Moon](https://bradymoon.com/) and Prof. [Sebastian Scherer](https://theairlab.org/team/sebastian/). At KTH, I worked on risk-aware motion planning and control with [Matti Vahs](https://www.kth.se/profile/vahs) and Prof. [Jana Tumova](https://sites.google.com/view/janatumova/home) in the Division of Robotics, Perception and Learning. I am also an organizer of the [RoboLaunch](https://riss.ri.cmu.edu/robolaunch/) outreach initiative that brings leading robotics and AI researcher around the world to present their work in accessible talks.

Besides those mentioned above, I am very grateful for the wonderful mentors and collaborators I've had including [Héctor Azpúrua](https://www.linkedin.com/in/hectorazp/), [Caio Conti](https://www.linkedin.com/in/caio-conti/) and [Rachel Burcin](https://www.rachelburcin.com/).

Apart from research, I enjoy playing and watching football :soccer:, mainly Flamengo games (the biggest team in Brazil as brilliantly [proved](https://youtu.be/Bk_2Oq41opY) by Bruno Henrique).
