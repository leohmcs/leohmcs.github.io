Hello! I am Leo, an Electrical Engineering student at [UFMG](https://ufmg.br/), in Brazil. I am currently in Stockholm, Sweden, for a semester abroad at [KTH Royal Institute of Technology](https://www.kth.se/en).

At UFMG, I am part of the Computer Vision and Robotics Lab ([VeRLab](https://www.verlab.dcc.ufmg.br/)), where I have been doing research in planning for multi-robot systems advised by Prof. [Douglas Macharet](https://homepages.dcc.ufmg.br/~doug/site/en/). Last summer, I worked as a Research Intern in the [AirLab](https://theairlab.org/) at Carnegie Mellon University as part of the Robotics Institute Summer Scholars ([RISS](https://riss.ri.cmu.edu/)) program advised by [Brady Moon](https://bradymoon.com/) and Prof. [Sebastian Scherer](https://theairlab.org/team/sebastian/).

Besides those mentioned above, I am very grateful for the wonderful mentors and collaborators I had including [Héctor Azpúrua](https://www.linkedin.com/in/hectorazp/), [Caio Conti](https://www.linkedin.com/in/caio-conti/) and [Rachel Burcin](https://www.rachelburcin.com/).

Apart from research, I enjoy playing and watching football, mainly Flamengo games (the biggest team in Brazil as brilliantly proved by [Bruno Henrique](https://youtu.be/Bk_2Oq41opY)).
